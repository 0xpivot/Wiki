## Conclusion

XXE injection is a serious vulnerability that can lead to significant security risks. By understanding how it works, identifying vulnerable parameters, and implementing proper defenses, you can protect your applications from XXE attacks. Always stay vigilant and keep your applications up-to-date with the latest security patches and configurations.

## Conclusion

XXE injection is a serious security vulnerability that can be exploited to read sensitive files, perform port scans, and interact with internal systems. By understanding the underlying mechanisms and implementing proper mitigation strategies, developers can effectively defend against XXE attacks and ensure the security of their applications.

## Practice Labs

For hands-on practice with business logic vulnerabilities, consider the following labs:

- **PortSwigger Web Security Academy**: Offers a variety of labs to practice exploiting and mitigating business logic vulnerabilities.
- **OWASP Juice Shop**: Provides a vulnerable web application to practice exploiting and mitigating various security vulnerabilities, including business logic vulnerabilities.

By completing these labs, you can gain practical experience in identifying and mitigating business logic vulnerabilities in web applications.

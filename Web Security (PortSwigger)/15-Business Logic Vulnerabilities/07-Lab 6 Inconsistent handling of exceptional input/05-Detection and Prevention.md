## Detection and Prevention

### Detection

Detection of business logic vulnerabilities involves thorough testing and monitoring of the application. Use automated tools and manual testing to identify potential issues.

### Prevention

Prevention involves implementing secure coding practices, validating user inputs, and enforcing business rules consistently.

### Secure Configuration Hardening

Hardening the application configuration can help prevent business logic vulnerabilities. Ensure that all configurations are secure and follow best practices.

### Real-World Examples and Case Studies

Real-world examples and case studies can provide valuable insights into business logic vulnerabilities. Analyze recent CVEs and breaches to understand the impact and learn from them.

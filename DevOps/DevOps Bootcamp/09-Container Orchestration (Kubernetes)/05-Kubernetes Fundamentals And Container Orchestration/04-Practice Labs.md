## Practice Labs

For hands-on practice with Kubernetes fundamentals and container orchestration, consider the following labs:

- **Kubernetes Goat**: A hands-on lab for learning Kubernetes security.
- **OWASP WrongSecrets**: A series of challenges to learn about secrets management in Kubernetes.
- **kube-hunter**: A tool for hunting vulnerabilities in Kubernetes clusters.

These labs will help you gain practical experience with the concepts discussed in this chapter.

## Conclusion

Authorization in Istio is a powerful mechanism for controlling access to services within a microservices architecture. By leveraging principles, negative configurations, and operation attributes, you can create fine-grained policies that ensure only authorized services can interact with each other. Regularly reviewing and updating these policies is crucial for maintaining a secure environment.

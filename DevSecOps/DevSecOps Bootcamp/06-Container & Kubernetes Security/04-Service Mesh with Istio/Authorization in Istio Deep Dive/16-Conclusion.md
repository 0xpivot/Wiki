## Conclusion

Authorization policies in Istio are a powerful tool for securing microservices-based applications. By defining granular rules for service-to-service communication, you can significantly reduce the attack surface and enhance overall security. Proper configuration and maintenance of these policies are essential to ensure that your services remain secure and resilient against potential threats.

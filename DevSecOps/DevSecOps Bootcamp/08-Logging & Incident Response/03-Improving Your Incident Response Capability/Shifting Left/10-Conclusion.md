## Conclusion

Shifting left is a critical principle in DevSecOps that emphasizes integrating security practices earlier in the SDLC. By incorporating security measures into every stage of the pipeline, teams can significantly improve their incident response capabilities and overall security posture. This proactive approach helps in detecting and mitigating security issues early, reducing the cost and complexity of fixing them later. By building learning into the pipeline, teams can continuously improve the security of their applications and create intellectual property within their build process.

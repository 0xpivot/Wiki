### Step 5: Training and Awareness

#### What is Training and Awareness?

Training and awareness involve educating team members about security best practices and the importance of following security policies. This helps in creating a security-conscious culture within the organization.

#### Why Training and Awareness?

Training and awareness ensure that team members are knowledgeable about security best practices and are able to identify and report potential security incidents. This helps in preventing incidents and responding to them effectively.

#### How to Implement Training and Awareness?

1. **Conduct Regular Training Sessions**: Organize regular training sessions to educate team members about security best practices.
2. **Provide Resources**: Provide resources such as documentation and guides to help team members understand security policies.
3. **Encourage Reporting**: Encourage team members to report potential security incidents and provide feedback on security policies.

#### Example Training Material

```markdown
# Secure Coding Best Practices

1. **Input Validation**: Always validate user input to prevent common vulnerabilities like SQL injection and XSS.
2. **Error Handling**: Handle errors gracefully to prevent information leakage.
3. **Least Privilege**: Grant the minimum necessary permissions to users and processes.
```

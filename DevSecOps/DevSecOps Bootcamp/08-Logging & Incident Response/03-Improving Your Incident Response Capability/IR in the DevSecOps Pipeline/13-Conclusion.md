## Conclusion

Integrating incident response into the DevSecOps pipeline is crucial for ensuring the security of systems and applications throughout their entire lifecycle. By implementing secure coding practices, continuous monitoring, and comprehensive incident response plans, organizations can reduce the likelihood and impact of security incidents. Regular training and awareness also play a vital role in creating a security-conscious culture within the organization.

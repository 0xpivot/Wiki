## Conclusion

In conclusion, the DevSecOps manifesto emphasizes the importance of business-driven security, red and blue team testing, proactive security monitoring, threat intelligence sharing, and compliance operations. These principles ensure that security is deeply integrated into the development and operational processes, providing a more robust and effective approach to securing modern systems. By following these principles, organizations can significantly improve their security posture and better protect their assets and customers.

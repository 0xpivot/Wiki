## Case Study: Gloomantics

### Background of Gloomantics

Gloomantics is a fictitious company that has been using an agile methodology for software development. They have some basic DevOps practices in place, particularly around automated and scripted deployments. However, their security practices are lacking, and they are open to trying new initiatives but constrained by limited resources. They also face tight release deadlines due to new market successes and customer wins.

### Ben's Role

Ben is a software developer at Gloomantics who wants to introduce DevSecOps practices into the company. His goal is to gain support from his manager and peers for this initiative. To achieve this, Ben needs to clearly articulate the benefits of DevSecOps and demonstrate how it can improve the overall security and efficiency of the development process.

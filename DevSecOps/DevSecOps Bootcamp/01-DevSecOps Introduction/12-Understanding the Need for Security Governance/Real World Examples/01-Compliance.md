#### Risk Management

Risk management is the process of identifying, assessing, and mitigating security risks. Key aspects include:

1. **Risk Assessment**: A process for identifying and evaluating potential security risks.
   
2. **Risk Mitigation**: Actions taken to reduce the likelihood or impact of identified risks.
   
3. **Risk Monitoring**: Ongoing monitoring of risks to ensure that they remain under control.

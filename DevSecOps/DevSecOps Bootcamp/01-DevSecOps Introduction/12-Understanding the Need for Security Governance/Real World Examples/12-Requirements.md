## Requirements

1. **Data Protection Officer (DPO)**: Appoint a DPO to oversee data protection activities.
2. **Privacy Impact Assessments (PIAs)**: Conduct PIAs for new projects and initiatives.
3. **Data Subject Rights**: Ensure that data subjects can exercise their rights, including the right to access, rectify, and delete their personal data.

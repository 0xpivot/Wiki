#### Monitoring and Auditing

Monitoring and auditing are essential for ensuring that security controls are effective and up-to-date. Key aspects include:

1. **Continuous Monitoring**: Regularly monitor systems for security events and anomalies.
   
2. **Internal Audits**: Conduct internal audits to assess the effectiveness of security controls.
   
3. **External Audits**: Engage external auditors to provide independent verification of security controls.

## Findings

1. **Weak Password Policies**: Employees are using weak passwords, increasing the risk of unauthorized access.
2. **Outdated Software**: Many systems are running outdated software, increasing the risk of vulnerabilities being exploited.
3. **Insufficient Access Controls**: Access controls are not consistently enforced, increasing the risk of unauthorized access.

## Roles and Responsibilities

- **Chief Information Officer (CIO)**: Responsible for overseeing the implementation and enforcement of this policy.
- **IT Security Team**: Responsible for monitoring compliance and conducting regular audits.
- **End Users**: Responsible for adhering to the guidelines outlined in this policy.

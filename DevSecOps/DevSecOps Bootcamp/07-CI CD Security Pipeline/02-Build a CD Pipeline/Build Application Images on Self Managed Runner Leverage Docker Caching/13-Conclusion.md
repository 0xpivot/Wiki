## Conclusion

Understanding and managing dangling Docker images and troubleshooting issues with self-managed runners are crucial aspects of maintaining a robust and efficient CI/CD pipeline. By regularly cleaning up dangling images and ensuring that your self-managed runners are running smoothly, you can prevent issues that can disrupt your development workflow. Additionally, implementing secure coding practices and robust error handling can further enhance the reliability and efficiency of your CI/CD pipeline.
